Driveway and paving refurbishment


Is your paving dirty, has weeds and needs a little TLC?. We can solve your maintenance problems.

We can transform your paving, bring it back to life and advice on simple long-term maintenance.

If your pattern imprinted concrete paving is more than three years old, it is likely to be in need of a spring clean. Once the paving has been thoroughly cleaned and treated for moss, algae or oil stains, a fresh coating of PICS High Solid Sealer will bring your paving alive again.

Maybe the drive was laid before you moved in, or perhaps it's just that you never really thought about long term maintenance? The facts are the same - wind, rain and the passing of time cause severe harm to block paved drives. Weeds establish themselves through the smallest of gaps and your once great looking paving needs attention.
